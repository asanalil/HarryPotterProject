Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone is the first film in the Harry Potter series. It was released in 2001 and is based on the novel by J.K. Rowling. The movie follows the adventures of a young wizard, Harry Potter, as he discovers his magical heritage and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets is the second film in the Harry Potter series. It was released in 2002 and continues the adventures of Harry Potter and his friends at Hogwarts. The film explores the mystery of the Chamber of Secrets and the dangers it holds.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban is the third film in the Harry Potter series. It was released in 2004 and is based on the novel by J.K. Rowling. The movie follows Harry's third year at Hogwarts and the escape of the dangerous prisoner Sirius Black.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire is the fourth film in the Harry Potter series. It was released in 2005 and is based on the novel by J.K. Rowling. The movie follows Harry's participation in the Triwizard Tournament and the return of the dark wizard Voldemort.

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix is the fifth film in the Harry Potter series. It was released in 2007 and is based on the novel by J.K. Rowling. The movie follows Harry's fifth year at Hogwarts and the formation of Dumbledore's Army to fight against the dark forces.

Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince

Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince is the sixth film in the Harry Potter series. It was released in 2009 and is based on the novel by J.K. Rowling. The movie explores Harry's journey to uncover the past of Voldemort and the discovery of Horcruxes.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows - Part 1

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows - Part 1 is the seventh film in the Harry Potter series. It was released in 2010 and is based on the novel by J.K. Rowling. The movie follows Harry, Ron, and Hermione as they embark on a quest to find and destroy the Horcruxes.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows - Part 2

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows - Part 2 is the eighth and final film in the Harry Potter series. It was released in 2011 and concludes the epic battle between Harry and Voldemort, the final showdown at Hogwarts, and the resolution of the series.
